My improvements include spatial attention gates and channel attention modules that significantly boost segmentation accuracy, especially for complex aerial scenes with multiple object classes.

Python TensorFlow U-Net Attention
🛰️ My Improvements ↓

🧬 Enhanced Architecture

🔍 Spatial Attention Gates for better feature localization
📡 Channel Attention Modules to focus on important features
🧊 Multi-scale feature fusion for complex aerial scenes
📊 Improved Jaccard coefficient over baseline U-Net
🌍 Better segmentation of buildings, roads, and vegetation
⚡ Efficient architecture maintaining real-time performance
